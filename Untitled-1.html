About the Film

A year after a secret party turned deadly, four high school students are drawn into a twisted game. Secrets resurface, trust breaks, and a masked figure known as "W." demands the truth—through fear, blood, and revenge.

Genre: Psychological Thriller / Mystery Teen Horror
Tone: Dark, creepy, dramatic, and shocking

Main Characters

Plot Summary

Act 1: One year after Lila’s mysterious death at a secret party, creepy messages appear. Someone knows what really happened—and starts tormenting the four friends.

Act 2: The game begins. If they don’t follow W.’s cruel tasks, someone innocent will suffer. They return to the abandoned theatre, uncovering bloody clues and deadly traps.

Act 3: They dig into Lila’s past and find a Cold War tunnel system under the school—and a darker conspiracy. They didn’t kill Lila, but they helped cover it up.

Act 4: At the Masquerade Ball, one of them dies. Another disappears. The nightmare intensifies.

Act 5: Blake reveals he is W. He didn’t just want revenge—he wanted Elena to confess. In a final act of violence, Jax saves her… but it’s not over.

Epilogue: Elena tries to move on… until she hears Lila’s voice one last time. “They’re still here. And they know everything.”
